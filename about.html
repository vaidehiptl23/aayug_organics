Our Story

Born from a passion for pure, organic food — Aayug Organics bridges the gap between Indian farms and your kitchen table.

🌿

From Farm to Your Family

Aayug Organics was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to pure, unadulterated food. In a market flooded with processed alternatives, we set out to bring back the authentic taste and nutrition of traditional Indian organic products.

Our journey began with A2 Gir Cow Ghee, sourced from indigenous Gir cows in Gujarat. The overwhelming response from health-conscious families inspired us to expand into raw honey, cold-pressed oils, and organic spices.

Today, we partner with over 50 trusted farmers across India, ensuring every product is sourced ethically and processed using traditional methods that preserve nutritional integrity.
